Mechanical Factory

The Steel Structural Division is well equipped with resources such as a working space of 10,000 square meters in two locations with approximately 6,300 square meters of overhead crane area. The Division also has dedicated abrasive blasting and painting areas. With a production capacity of 500 MT/Months, the Division is managed by a group of more than 300 qualified and dedicated personnel.

 

The services provided by the Division range from structural steelworks; fabrication of vessels and tanks; piping works; furnace, stacks, silos, canopies; sheds, gates and doors;

special coatings; shearing, bending and machining; shutdown maintenance and general fabrication.

 

• Fabrication Working Area: 10000 m2 (two locations)
• Overhead Crane Area: 4000 m2
• Abrasive Blasting Area: 100 m2
• Painting Area: 100 m2

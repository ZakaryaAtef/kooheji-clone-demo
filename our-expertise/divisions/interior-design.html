Kooheji Interiors

Kooheji Interiors Studio was established to deliver exceptional design and build solutions where aesthetics meets budget.

 

Our creative team of exceptionally talented designers brings an intensive experience of delivering world-class mega projects across the Kingdom of Bahrain.

 

Providing a wide range of services, Kooheji Interiors carries out space planning, concept design, aesthetic design, detailed construction drawings, project management, furniture manufacturing and procurement.

 

At Kooheji Interiors, we strive to deliver a seamless customer experience where Interior design, project management, project execution, joinery factory production, furniture manufacturing, and procurement work all in parallel to deliver exceptional client experience and tailor-made bespoke furniture solutions that meet clients' budgets and exceed their expectations.

 

With unmatched elegance and a touch of class, we are interior specialists in fit-out, joinery, furniture, furnishing & case goods. We provide turnkey interior fit-out and contracting solutions to luxury hospitality, prime residential, commercial sectors, palaces and high-profile residences.

 

At Kooheji Interiors, we've positioned ourselves at the pinnacle of hospitality design. By blending moving aesthetics with brand identity, we're redefining the guest experience. Our collaborative approach, powered by 50 years of industry insights and a technology-backed structure, enables us to push boundaries consistently.

 

Global Presence: Spanning decades, we've cultivated a network of over 1,500 prequalified suppliers worldwide, ensuring efficient project management across various scales.

Technology - KCProcure: Transparency is paramount. Our state-of-the-art software facilitates rapid report generation, covering all procurement phases from the BOQ stage to the handover.

Our Team: Global, creative, and goal-oriented. We are driven by bold solutions and big thinking, ensuring each project we undertake resonates with quality and innovation.

 

Procurement Management:

• Design, Development & Planning: We dive deep into our client's vision, aligning design with intent and ensuring cost-effective strategies.

• Procurement: Leveraging our global supplier network, we negotiate optimal terms and conditions. Our focus is on quality assurance, vetting suppliers diligently for service consistency and timeliness.

• Logistics: We partner with trusted logistics firms, ensuring timely and cost-effective deliveries.

• Installation & Handover: Our commitment extends to ensuring all procured items are appropriately installed and functional.

 

Joinery, FF&E, and OS&E:

• FF&E (Furniture, Fixtures & Equipment): We either source specified products or collaborate with interior designers to identify suitable alternatives.

• OS&E (Operating Supplies and Equipment): Essential for space functionality, we procure these keeping in sync with brand standards.

 

Strategic Contracting: From hospitality to retail, our contracting encompasses diverse projects, offering turnkey solutions from inception to installation.

 

Direct Access & Project Management: By eliminating middlemen and leveraging our global operations, we guarantee efficient project deliveries and cost savings.

 

Pre-Opening Activation & Pathway Development: Our expertise ensures optimal pre-opening budgets, aligned with brand standards and SOPs.

 

F&B Concept Development:

• Concept Brief: Collaborative in approach, we define brand goals and translate them into tangible project elements.

• Concept Design & Brand Development: From strategy to visual identity, we craft unique F&B experiences.

• Activation Support: We convert F&B concepts to reality, overseeing all pre-opening activities.

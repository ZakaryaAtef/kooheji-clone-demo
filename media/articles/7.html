ONYX SKYVIEW Groundbreaking Ceremony 2024

Kooheji Development Announced a Groundbreaking Ceremony to Mark the Start of Construction of its second project of the ONYX series: “ONYX SKYVIEW”

Kooheji Development, Bahrain’s leading real state Company and Kooheji Contractors, has proudly initiated construction on "Onyx SkyView" - the second project in their esteemed ONYX series.

Kooheji Contractors Achieves 10% Construction Milestone: A Step Forward in Realizing the Unique Architectural Vision in the Heart of Bahrain Bay

 

In a significant step that highlights its commitment to pioneering residential luxury, Kooheji Development, Bahrain’s leading real state Company and Kooheji Contractors, has proudly initiated construction on "Onyx SkyView" - the second project in their esteemed ONYX series. Situated in the dynamic heart of Bahrain Bay, this latest addition to the ONYX series promises to transform the skyline with an unmatched luxury design. The groundbreaking ceremony, attended by the company's Board of Directors and prominent stakeholders, signifies a new chapter in the distinguished trajectory of Kooheji Development and its construction affiliate, paving the way for another architectural masterpiece in Bahrain.

Kooheji Contractors has been assigned as the main contractor of the "Onyx SkyView" project, and with the start of the construction activities, Kooheji Development is now one step closer to making this unique and exciting development a reality in Bahrain. "Onyx SkyView" will bring a new paradigm for elegant high-rise luxury development in Bahrain and the region of creating a modern and sustainable living experience. Additionally, the project focuses on creating an inviting and comfortable atmosphere for its residents, with ample green spaces, comfortable living spaces, and modern amenities. With these elements, "Onyx SkyView" will create a luxurious living experience that is both modern and elegant.

 

Featuring a grand main entrance facing the Four Seasons hotel, "Onyx SkyView" spreads over construction area of 108,000 sqm and comprises luxury residences, offices as well as an attractive retail unit along the two sides of the project. The project consists of 435 luxury residential apartments distributed over 53 floors, in addition to 40 offices distributed over 10 floors with four offices on each floor and seven commercial stores.

           

The spacious and ultra-luxury residences at Onyx SkyView are a carefully curated collection of studios, one-, two- and 3.5-bedroom apartments combining distinctive design, and inspired lifestyle inclusions in the best layouts and space planning within the Kooheji Development residential towers portfolio.

 

 

"Onyx SkyView" will be nestled in spacious grounds complete with architectural terraces of green outdoor spaces and features dedicated residents-only amenities in a double-height indoor floor as well as outdoors over a 3,800-sq-m area.  These resort-style indoor and outdoor amenities include active and passive entertainment activities zones, such as an indoor multipurpose hall, VIP lounge, café, cinema theatre, ladies' and men’s gymnasiums, play areas as well as outdoor water features including levelled decks with iconic waterfall cascades between pools as well as fitness areas with lap infinity pool, dynamic splash pads, and a lazy river surrounded by fire pits spots for the cozy family barbecue gathering.

 

Once fully operational, the project will provide a luxurious and resort-like community with unique amenities close to some of Bahrain's most renowned attractions. These attractions include the Bahrain Bay promenade, five-star hotels, the Gardens, and the seafronts. This project will provide an opportunity to experience the best that Bahrain offers in one convenient location.

 

The floor-to-ceiling windows of the residences offer panoramic views of Bahrain's skyline from the bedrooms and living spaces. New classic wooden flooring designs, cool stone finishes and panelled walls bring a natural touch to balance the impact of the expansive glazing. The spacious floor plans can meet various customers' preferences and can be easily configured into a range of different requirements. In addition, optional and extra interior design packages will also be available to buyers. The mixed-use development also benefits from proximity to major shopping malls and is within walking distance of the buzzing life of Bahrain Bay.

 

Commenting from his side, Eng. Mohamed Abdulghaffar AlKooheji, CEO of Kooheji Development said: “We are happy to celebrate today the ground-breaking of the "Onyx SkyView" project, which with Allah’s blessings will be an iconic project in Bahrain’s skyline. We are also glad to announce that Kooheji Contractors is the main contractor for the project. With their expertise and experience in the construction industry, we are certain that this project will be another success story of Kooheji's legacy. Our combined experience of 50 years in the real estate and construction sectors, and in particular the development of high-rise luxury residential towers, will ensure that this project is completed with the highest quality standards and high-end detail finishes. . To achieve the redefinition of the luxury of living in high-rise residential towers.

“We would like to extend our sincerest best wishes to the team working on this project. We understand the hard work and dedication that has gone into each project, and we wish them all the best of luck throughout the project journey. We hope for successful completion and are confident that the team will do an amazing job” added Eng. Mohamed Al Kooheji.

As we forge ahead with the construction of "Onyx SkyView," Kooheji Contractors has now reached a significant milestone with 10% of the construction work complete. This progress is a testament to our unwavering dedication to excellence and our commitment to bringing this visionary project to life. The completed phases of construction so far include the completion of site mobilization, extensive excavation work, and the commencement of structured piling. This rapid development is a clear indication of our expertise in managing and executing large-scale projects with precision and efficiency.

Kooheji Development is further solidifying its position as a leader in the development of luxury high-rise residential towers in Bahrain. The company's track record of projects executed totals 14 projects, making it the largest player in the real estate sector in Bahrain and one of the largest in the region. This project reinforces Kooheji Development's standing as a major player in the industry.

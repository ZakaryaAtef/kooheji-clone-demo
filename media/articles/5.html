Kooheji Contractors celebrates 50th Anniversary

Kooheji Contractors & Development, a prominent construction & Renowned leading developer in Bahrain, marked its 50th anniversary with a special celebration for its employees. The event, held at the Gulf Convention Center in Manama, Bahrain, kicked off with a commemoration of the company's golden jubilee.

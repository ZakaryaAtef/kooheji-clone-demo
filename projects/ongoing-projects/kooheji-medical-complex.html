Kooheji Medical Complex

Kooheji Private Medical Complex is a four-storey complex located at the edge of Salmaniya Complex serving as an extension to the current Salmaniya Medical Complex and this will increase the capacity and well-being of the visitors/patients. It includes 150 car parking with spacious drop-off areas to the entrances with comfortable ramps to ensure all standards are met. The architecture of the complex follows a simple modern geometric design with beautiful stone cladding and recesses of grey plaster finish, in addition to large double-glazed windows overlooking the lavish green landscape to brighten the building and create a healing experience for all the users. The space planning of the interior spaces is functionally designed to give convenience and comfort to all the users including the medical staff, patients, and visitors. The experience starts at the ground floor entrance with a large open space lobby, café, cafeteria, prayer rooms, and spacious waiting area in the middle to give an uplifting feeling and add convenience to the circulation of the users. The other part of the ground floor includes fully equipped 10 clinics, a phlebotomy lab, and high-end radiology rooms (MRI/CT Scan/ X-Ray).

The first floor will be composed of 7 clinics, 2 operation theaters, endoscopy rooms, and 3 well-equipped ICU Rooms. In addition, the second floor will be composed of 2 delivery rooms with a nursery for newborns and most of the second-floor space will be dedicated to patient rooms divided into 6 inpatient private Rooms and 5 spacious Deluxe rooms. Lastly, the third floor will accommodate an additional 4 Inpatient private rooms and 6 luxurious VIP suites. All the patient rooms are beautifully naturally lighted with views giving a healing experience.

 

The complex follows all safety and medical standards to ensure a wholesome healing experience with well-studied entrances/exits, strategic vertical circulation, safe fire exit pathways, well-equipped labs, high-end radiology rooms, comfortable patient rooms, and all that a high-end medical complex would need. Kooheji Private Medical Complex will be a valuable asset to the Kingdom of Bahrain and especially to the Salmaniya complex as it will help to increase Bahrain’s capacity to heal patients and create a higher well-being for all the users.

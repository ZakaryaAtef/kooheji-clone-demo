Vision 2030 & Tharwa: Building the Dream

 

Saudi Arabia's Vision 2030 is a bold blueprint for the future, aiming to create a thriving nation where dreams, hopes, and ambitions come to life. Central to this vision is the goal of ensuring 70% homeownership by 2030. At the heart of this transformative journey lies Tharwa, a town that epitomizes the spirit of this vision. Belgravia, our latest residential project, is our humble contribution towards this national dream. It's a commitment to luxury, comfort, and unparalleled lifestyle.

Where Classic Meets Contemporary

 

Belgravia, set against the picturesque backdrop of Tharwa, is an architectural marvel that bridges the gap between classical English aesthetics and modern design elements. With four distinct buildings surrounded by lush English landscapes, residents enjoy panoramic views of the sea, city, and central courtyard. From golden railings and intricate corniches to expansive double-glazed windows, Belgravia promises an experience that is as opulent as it is homely. The blend of traditional craft with modern engineering is evident, making Belgravia a beacon of style and sophistication.

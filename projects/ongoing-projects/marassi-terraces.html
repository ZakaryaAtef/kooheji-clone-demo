Marassi Terraces

Kooheji Contractors holds the esteemed position as the Main Contractor for the Marassi Terraces Project, developed by the renowned Bahraini real estate firm, Eagle Hills Diyar Co WLL.

 

Ideally located in Diyar Al Muharraq, the project is positioned in the heart of the "Marassi" Master plan. The project consists of two 10-storey residential buildings, equipped with shops, cafes, and restaurants on the ground floor, a podium with amenities like a swimming pool, and ample basement parking.

 

The innovative design includes terraces, private balconies, and a unique view of Marassi Galleria. The serene water-filled gardens, cascading waterfalls, and canal walkways make it a perfect harmony of luxury and nature.

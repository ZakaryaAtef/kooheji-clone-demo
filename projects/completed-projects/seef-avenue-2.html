SEEF AVENUE II

Seef Avenue II is a residential tower developed by Kooheji Development, comprising 30-storey spread over a built-up area of approximately 35,000 square meters. The project includes a basement, ground floor, 2-level parking, and 27 typical floors. With a total of 176 luxurious apartment units, ranging from studios to three-bedroom apartments, the project offers a multitude of living options. Amenities such as a well-equipped fitness center, an opulent outdoor pool, sauna & steam facilities, a co-working space, children's play area, meeting room, multipurpose hall, executive lounge, and BBQ area elevate the living experience.

Construction Progress Update:

 

As of June 2023, Kooheji contractors as the main contractor of the project is delighted to report significant advancements in the Seef Avenue II project. Within less than a year since its launch in October 2022, the project has astoundingly achieved 61% construction completion. This rapid progression is testament to Kooheji ‘s commitment to timely delivery and excellence. Highlighting the construction milestones, there's notable structural progress from the 28th to 29th floors, substantial blockwork and plastering on the 4th floor Parking Level, and continuous advancements in interior works. Seef Avenue II is swiftly shaping up to be a beacon of luxury and architectural prowess in Bahrain.

Fontana Infinity

Kooheji Contractors was appointed as the main contractor for the design and build of Fontana Infinity. The project consists of two towers (Tower A 45 floors and Tower B 46 floors). Total built-up area is approximately 130,000 square meters with a ground floor, a mezzanine floor, and 5-level parking. It has 853 various types of luxurious apartment units ranging from studios, single 

bedroom, two-bedroom and three-bedroom apartments. Podium floors consist of a large main swimming pool, indoor swimming pool, multi-purpose hall, business centre, conference room, steam, sauna rooms including spa and jacuzzi.

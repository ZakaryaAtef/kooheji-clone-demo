About Kooheji Contractors

A track record of delivering over 6100 Projects

Delivering World-Class Projects

A detailed and methodical approach combined with a deep insight to market expectations, gained through many years of successful construction project experience, allows us to deliver outstanding projects to our clients. Our ability to transfer knowledge between different geographic markets also contributes to the outcomes we build.

 

Synonymous with integrity, trust and fairness, a driving element to our success lies in our values and code of conduct, which includes policies on employee relations, health & safety, the environment and business ethics. We believe that addressing quality, health & safety, and environmental issues leads to success in the construction industry. A philosophy that has

seen by Kooheji Contractors being the first construction company in Bahrain to implement the Integrated Management System (IMS), with certification to ISO 9001:2015, ISO 14001:2015, and ISO 45001:2018 standards.

 

Through our customer-focused operations, we balance the interests of owners and customers; our employees, suppliers, subcontractors and the wider community – through understanding current needs and anticipating future aspirations. We work within a set framework of interlinked processes, all aimed at continuous improvement and thus enhancing our relationships with all our stakeholders and, of course, the clients we serve.

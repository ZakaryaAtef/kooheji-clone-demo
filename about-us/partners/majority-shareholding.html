Paschal Concrete Formwork Co. W.L.L., Bahrain

Paschal Concrete Formwork Co. W.L.L. was established as a subsidiary of Kooheji Contractor with a controlling interest of 51% and 49% participation of Paschal Work G. Maier Gmb, Germany. Paschal is one of the leading formwork manufacturers in the world and has more than 30 years business experience in the Middle East. The modular system is the most popular formwork in the Middle East among the other products of Paschal.

The Developers W.L.L., Bahrain

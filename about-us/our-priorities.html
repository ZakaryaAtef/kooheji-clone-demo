Integrated Management System (IMS)

Kooheji Contractors is the first contracting company to secure an ISO 9001 for Quality in Bahrain. We are committed to delivering our projects with the highest commitment to Quality, Health, Safety and Environment compliance and believe that quality shapes success. To pursue this goal and to provide the necessary confidence to our clients, we operate to an Integrated Management System which is accredited to:   

i. Quality Management System ISO 9001:2015  

ii. Environmental Management System ISO 14001:2015 and  

iii. Occupational Health & Safety Management System OHSAS 45001:2018

Quality Standards

The Company has developed quality management systems that best suit its modes of operation and remain user-friendly. We prepare and issue quality control plans, work procedures and work instructions through rigorous control systems to ensure that our corporate quality management system is met - in line with the project scope based on the client requirements.

Health, Safety and Environment Management

Kooheji Contractors understand the significance of health and safety and environmental protection and ensure all measures to protect the health and safety of our employees and the environment while complying with applicable national laws and ministerial decrees. The company trains every employee to understand that safety always comes first.

With a zero-tolerance to lack of safety, we have developed a culture under-written by a world-class Occupational Health & Safety and Environmental program accredited to OHSAS 14001:2015 and ISO 45001:2018. This promotes an injury-free working environment and provides the safest workplace possible for our employees, subcontractors, clients and others who enter our work sites.

All management and personnel at Kooheji Contractors are fully committed to performing business in the safest manner possible embracing international construction practices and, as a result, the Company has consistently delivered its projects without lost-time accidents.

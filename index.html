Welcome to Kooheji Contractors

Established in 1973, Kooheji Contractors has over 50 years of experience in construction, civil engineering and design, with an outstanding record of success both in Bahrain and throughout the MENA region.

Our construction experience ranges from urban development to commercial and industrial projects contributing to growing the quality of a community lifestyle through affordable housing projects, waterfront communities, luxury high-rise living and more.
